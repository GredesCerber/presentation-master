What is Cybersecurity?

The practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information.

— NIST Framework

Confidentiality

Protecting data from unauthorized access

Incident Response Process

1. Preparation

Develop IR plan, train team, establish communication channels, deploy tools

2. Detection

Monitor systems, analyze alerts, identify indicators of compromise

3. Containment

Isolate affected systems, prevent spread, preserve evidence

4. Eradication

Remove malware, close vulnerabilities, strengthen defenses

5. Recovery

Restore systems, verify integrity, return to operations

6. Lessons Learned

Document incident, update procedures, improve defenses
